ANGELA'S RECIPE FOR THE BEST CINNAMON ROLLS

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons groun cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast,sugar,eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. Kernel the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plasticwrap.
    leaver it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
